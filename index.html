What is Factorio?

Factorio is a game about building and managing factories on an alien planet. You start with basic tools and raw materials, then progressively unlock technology, craft increasingly complex production chains, and automate everything. The core gameplay loop is addictive: gather resources, build machines, create conveyor systems, and watch your factory grow exponentially. From simple furnaces to massive mega-factories with thousands of production lines, Factorio challenges you to optimize every system. The game demands strategic thinking, planning, and creative problem-solving as you work toward launching a satellite and escaping the hostile planet.

"The factory must grow. Production never stops."

ESSENTIAL GUIDES

GATHERING BASICS

Start by gathering wood and stone with your hands. Craft a wooden axe and pickaxe to speed up collection. Build your first furnace to smelt copper and iron ore.

FIRST MACHINES

Craft wooden chests to organize your inventory. Build a basic conveyor belt system to automate ore smelting. Create your first assemblers.

STEAM POWER

Build boilers and steam engines to generate electricity. Create a coal-powered steam system to run your early machines. Design your first electrical grid.

DEFENSE SETUP

Craft walls and gun turrets to defend against biters. Establish a perimeter around your factory. Build ammo production lines.

OIL PROCESSING

Research oil processing and build your first refinery. Create chemical plants to process crude oil into fuel and sulfuric acid. Establish long supply chains for petroleum products.

NUCLEAR POWER

Build uranium mining and enrichment systems. Construct nuclear reactors for massive power generation. Design heat exchanger systems for efficient steam production.

LOGISTICS NETWORK

Design logistic networks with provider and requester chests. Create robot highways using roboports. Establish efficient supply distribution across your factory.

RATIOS & BALANCING

Master production ratios for efficient crafting. Balance input and output to avoid bottlenecks. Optimize assembler placement and speed modules.

MEGABASE PLANNING

Design modular factory layouts for massive scale production. Plan bus systems for centralized resource distribution. Build specialized production zones for each product line.

SPACE EXPLORATION

Launch rockets to space platforms. Build orbital stations and mining outposts. Establish space-based production chains. Begin interplanetary resource management.

ALIEN COMBAT

Research military technologies and defense turrets. Build walls and automated defense systems. Manage biters with pollution control. Establish secure perimeter around factory.

ENDGAME GOALS

Set ambitious production targets (Thousands Per Minute). Achieve space elevator completion. Optimize complex multi-level supply chains. Master the ultimate factory challenge.

BLUEPRINT MASTERY

Create reusable blueprint books for common production lines. Share blueprints with other players. Use construction robots to build from blueprints instantly. Blueprints save countless hours.

TRAIN SYSTEMS

Build efficient train networks for long-distance transport. Program train schedules and stations correctly. Design train unloading stations for maximum throughput. Trains handle massive volumes.

DEBUGGING FACTORIES

Use production graphs to identify bottlenecks. Monitor item flows to spot inefficiencies. Check power consumption for electrical issues. Systematic debugging solves problems quickly.

COMMUNITY & MODS

Explore popular community mods to enhance gameplay. Join multiplayer servers to play with friends. Share your factory designs with the community. Factorio's modding scene expands content.
